Crystals Collector

The point to this game is to try and match Your Score to the Random Number. Each crystal contains a different hidden number, when clicked this number will be added to your score. If you match your score with the Random Number, you win. If your score exceeds the Random Number, you lose. Click "Play Again" to reset the game but keep your score, click "Reset Game" to reset the whole game.
